# call-center-data-analysis# 

## Overview

The **Call Center Data Analysis Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of call center performance, customer satisfaction, sentiment trends, operational metrics, and geographical distribution of calls. The dashboard helps managers and stakeholders monitor service quality, identify customer concerns, and optimize support operations.

---

# Dashboard Objectives

* Monitor overall call volume and call duration.
* Analyze customer satisfaction (CSAT) scores.
* Understand customer sentiment distribution.
* Identify major reasons for customer contacts.
* Track call activity trends over time.
* Analyze call distribution by channel, city, and state.
* Support data-driven decision-making for service improvement.

---

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. Total Calls

**Metric:** 32.94K

Represents the total number of calls handled during the reporting period.

**Purpose:**

* Measure call center workload.
* Track customer engagement volume.

---

### 2. Average Call Duration

**Metric:** 25.02 Minutes

Represents the average duration of customer calls.

**Purpose:**

* Evaluate agent efficiency.
* Identify complex customer issues.

---

### 3. Average CSAT Score

**Metric:** 2.07 / 5

Measures overall customer satisfaction based on post-call surveys.

**Purpose:**

* Assess service quality.
* Monitor customer experience trends.

# Business Insights

### Customer Experience

* Overall CSAT score is relatively low (2.07), indicating opportunities for service improvement.
* High negative sentiment suggests customer dissatisfaction.

### Operational Performance

* Billing-related issues dominate customer contacts.
* Consistent call volumes allow predictable workforce scheduling.

### Geographic Trends

* California and Texas represent major customer service demand centers.
* City-level analysis highlights regions requiring focused support.

### Channel Performance

* Traditional call center remains the preferred support channel.
* Digital channels offer opportunities for self-service expansion.

---

# Recommendations

### Improve Billing Processes

* Investigate common billing complaints.
* Enhance billing transparency and communication.

### Reduce Negative Sentiment

* Analyze negative call transcripts.
* Implement targeted agent training programs.

### Increase Customer Satisfaction

* Improve first-call resolution rates.
* Reduce wait times and transfer rates.

### Optimize Resource Allocation

* Schedule agents based on peak call volume trends.
* Allocate additional support to high-demand states and cities.

### Strengthen Digital Support Channels

* Expand chatbot capabilities.
* Improve web self-service options to reduce call volume.
